<p><p>The aerospace carbon fiber composite market consists of two main types: thermosetting and thermoplastic. Thermosetting composites are rigid and heat-resistant, making them suitable for structural applications in aerospace. They are created by heating a mixture of carbon fibers and a resin, which hardens irreversibly upon curing. Thermoplastic composites, on the other hand, can be melted and reshaped multiple times. They offer improved impact resistance and can be manufactured efficiently. Both types have unique properties that make them ideal for various aerospace applications.</p></p>
