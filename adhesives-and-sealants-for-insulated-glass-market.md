<p><p>Adhesives and sealants for insulated glass are specifically designed to bond and seal the glass panes used in double or triple glazed windows. Firstly, the increasing demand for energy-efficient buildings is fueling the adoption of insulated glass, which in turn supports the demand for adhesives and sealants. Insulated glass helps in reducing energy consumption by regulating indoor temperature, resulting in cost savings and environmental benefits.</p><p>Additionally, the rising trend of green buildings and stringent regulations regarding energy efficiency are expected to contribute to market growth. Adhesives and sealants that are eco-friendly and have low volatile organic compound (VOC) content are gaining popularity in the industry.</p><p>The current outlook of the adhesives and sealants for insulated glass market is also promising.
