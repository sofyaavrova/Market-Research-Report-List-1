<p><p>The Automotive Motor Igniting Coil Market consists of two types: Open Magnetic Circuit Type Igniting Coil and Closed Magnetic Circuit Type Igniting Coil. </p><p>Open Magnetic Circuit Type Igniting Coil is designed with an open magnetic field, providing better heat dissipation. It is more cost-effective and compact but has a limited ability to generate high voltage.</p><p>Closed Magnetic Circuit Type Igniting Coil, on the other hand, has a closed magnetic field that enhances its ability to generate higher voltage, resulting in improved spark energy. It is larger and more expensive but offers better overall performance and durability.</p></p>
