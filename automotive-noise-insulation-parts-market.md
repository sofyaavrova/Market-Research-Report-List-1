<p><p>The automotive noise insulation parts market consists of various types of insulation components that help reduce noise in vehicles. Two common types include dash insulators and hood insulators. Dash insulators are used to reduce noise from the engine and road while also providing thermal insulation. They are typically made of materials like foam or rubber and are installed on the dashboard. Hood insulators, on the other hand, are designed to minimize engine noise and vibrations. They are placed underneath the hood to absorb sound and protect the engine from excessive heat.</p></p>
