<p><p>The connector assembly machines market can be classified into three types - semi-automatic, automatic, and manual. Automatic machines, on the other hand, are fully automated and perform all the assembly tasks without any human intervention. They have higher efficiency and productivity compared to semi-automatic machines. Manual machines involve complete human effort in assembling connectors, making them less efficient and time-consuming. These different types cater to varying requirements and budgets of manufacturers in the connector assembly industry.</p></p>
