There are four main types of functionalized polyolefins in the market. Randomly functionalized polyolefins have functional groups distributed randomly along the polymer chain. End functionalized polyolefins have functional groups located only at the chain ends. Block functionalized polyolefins have functional groups arranged in blocks along the polymer chain. Graft functionalized polyolefins have functional groups grafted onto the polymer backbone. Each type offers specific properties and applications in various industries.</p></p>
