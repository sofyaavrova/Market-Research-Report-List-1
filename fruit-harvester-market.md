<p><p>The fruit harvester market can be classified into three types: self-propelled, trailed, and mounted fruit harvesters. Self-propelled fruit harvesters are equipped with their own engine and can move independently. Trailed fruit harvesters are towed by another vehicle and operated through a power take-off shaft. Mounted fruit harvesters are attached to the rear or front of a tractor and are powered by the tractor's engine. These different types cater to different farming needs and allow farmers to efficiently harvest fruits in large quantities.</p></p>
