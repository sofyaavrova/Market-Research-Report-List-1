<p><p>Microphone phantom adapters are devices used to provide power (usually 48v) to condenser microphones that require it to function properly. There are two main types of microphone phantom adapters: in-line and embedded. </p><p>In-line adapters are separate devices that are connected between the microphone and the audio interface or mixer. They are portable and can be easily swapped between different microphones.</p><p>On the other hand, embedded adapters are built directly into the audio interface or mixer, eliminating the need for a separate device. This integration offers convenience and saves space, but it also means that the adapter is fixed to the specific equipment.</p></p>
