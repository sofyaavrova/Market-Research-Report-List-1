<p><p>Microprocessor protectors refer to devices used to safeguard microprocessors from damage caused by power surges, voltage fluctuations, short circuits, and electrical faults. MCB (Miniature Circuit Breakers) are automatic switches that detect and interrupt excessive current flow to protect the microprocessor. ELCB (Earth Leakage Circuit Breakers) continuously monitor and interrupt power supply if any leakage current to earth is detected, preventing electric shock hazards. Other types of protectors in this market include surge protectors, voltage stabilizers, and power conditioners that protect microprocessors by regulating voltage levels and suppressing electrical surges.</p></p>
