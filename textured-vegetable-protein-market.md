<p><p>The Textured Vegetable Protein (TVP) market comprises different types, including Protein Concentrate, Protein Isolate, Textured Protein, and Soy Flour. Protein Concentrate is obtained by removing water-soluble carbohydrates and fibers, resulting in a higher protein content. Protein Isolate undergoes further processing to remove non-protein components, resulting in a purer form of protein. Textured Protein undergoes a process where soy flour is extruded, creating a meat-like texture. Soy Flour is produced by grinding dehulled soybean flakes into a fine powder, which has various applications in baking, snacks, and infant formulas.</p></p>
