<p><p>The elastomeric thermal rubber market can be classified into different types based on the materials used. NBR based rubber refers to those made from nitrile butadiene rubber, which possess excellent oil resistance. EPDM based rubber is made from ethylene propylene diene monomer and is known for its superior resistance to weathering, sunlight, and ozone. Chloroprene based rubber is made from chloroprene and offers good resistance to fire and chemicals. The "others" category includes rubber types such as silicone and fluorocarbon, which have their own unique properties and applications.</p></p>
