<p><p>The recycled PE market is classified into two main types: recycled LDPE and recycled HDPE. It is commonly used in various applications such as film packaging and plastic bags. Recycled HDPE, on the other hand, is polyethylene plastic derived from recycled containers like milk jugs and shampoo bottles. It finds application in products such as plastic lumber, drainage pipes, and plastic bottles. Both recycled LDPE and recycled HDPE contribute to reducing plastic waste and promoting environmental sustainability.</p></p>
